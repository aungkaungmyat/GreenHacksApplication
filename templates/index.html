What is GreenHacks?

GreenHacks will tackle the growing problem of a lack of qualified individuals for technology related jobs. Participants will have the opportunity to learn about all the new inspiring technologies and apply new and old skills to build remarkable projects. A special focus will be placed on promoting solutions to the environmental issues that are plaguing our country. To help bring awareness and solutions to the environmental epidemic, we will be offering a prize for the best project related to the environment. We will also be offering prizes to the top 3 projects regardless of topic. Company specific awards will also be given. The event will last 36 hours, giving plenty of time for participants to collaborate with teammates to complete projects and share skills. There will also be plenty of time for networking as participants will be encouraged to talk freely with other participants or any of the participating companies. In this day and age, it is important for the general public to be familiar and comfortable with technology. By encouraging a positive environment for growth and learning, participants will actively reach out for advice and network among other peers.
